My name is Conner Smith

I am an indie game developer who is specialising in gameplay programming. I currently specialise in the Unity Engine and use c# as my primary programming language. I also have experience with programming in Python as well as basic training in Maya and Blender for 3D modelling and animation. I have chosen to specialise in this field because of my natural curiosity towards the creation process of many memorable mechanics such as the portal gun from Portal or the dead eye mechanic from Red Dead Redemption.
